ABOUT SMILE CATTERY

A Warm Welcome Awaits

Finding the right cattery is about more than simply choosing somewhere for your cat to stay. It's about finding people you can trust to care for them as if they were their own.

Hello and welcome!

Thank you for visiting Smile Cattery at Lightwood. Since 2015, we've provided loving, professional care in a peaceful setting just outside Sheffield.

Every cat is different — some settle in straight away, others need a little more time — so we care for each guest as an individual. Many return year after year, and we love seeing familiar faces.

OUR PROMISE

Individual Care, Always

No two cats are the same, so we get to know every guest and care for them in the way that suits them best — following feeding instructions and giving medication where needed, at no extra cost.

WHATEVER THE WEATHER

Comfort All Year Round

We adapt the cattery all year round, so every guest stays comfortable — whatever the British weather is doing.

Summer Comfort

Cooling gel mats, cool-touch bedding, frozen treats, extra fresh water and plenty of airflow all help our guests stay comfortable during warmer weather.

Keeping Things Cool

Curtains are repositioned to reduce direct sunshine, fans help circulate fresh air and we continually adapt each suite as temperatures change.

Cosy Winters

Heated sleeping areas, insulated beds and warm blankets provide cosy hideaways throughout the colder months.

Always Adapting

Every cat is different, so we continually adjust bedding, enrichment and the environment depending on both the weather and your cat's individual needs.

🐾 Fun fact: On warm days, you might spot a bag of frozen peas tucked under a blanket — just one of the little ways we help our guests keep cool!
