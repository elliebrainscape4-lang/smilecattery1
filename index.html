Welcome to Smile Cattery

Welcome to Smile Cattery at Lightwood. Under proprietorship since June 2015, formerly Atkinson's Cattery, we provide loving, professional care in a peaceful countryside setting just 3.5 miles from Sheffield city centre.

Situated at Mansion House Farm in the beautiful Moss Valley, our spacious heated walk-in pens offer cats a relaxing holiday while you're away.

We are fully International Cat Care (ICC) trained, cat owners ourselves, and understand that every guest deserves patient, individual care.
